Builds for Fishing

Fishing is a small game about a fisherman in ancient Greece. It's currently in development by me, Thomas Viktil, and it's open source. These are the published builds of the game.

Whenever I feel I have something to show, I deploy a build to this page.

I post updates on Twitter (@mandarinx), and you can follow, star or fork the entire project at GitHub.

The game is made with JavaScript and Phaser on a Node.js server.
